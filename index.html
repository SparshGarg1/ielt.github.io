TOEFL

This test preparation course, developed by the experts who create, administer and score the TOEFL iBT test, will help English language learners improve their skills. The TOEFL iBT test is the world’s most trusted and widely accepted English-language assessment. It has helped millions of people achieve their dreams to study, work or live abroad. More than 11,500 universities, government agencies and other institutions in over 160 countries worldwide accept TOEFL iBT scores for making important decisions.
